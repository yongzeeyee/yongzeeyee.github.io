Top Trend Colour of Baju Kurung in 2023

Baju Kurung, or traditional Malay attire worn during the festive season of Hari Raya Aidilfitri, is often a reflection of the wearer's personality and style. Whether you prefer traditional or modern designs, the colours you choose can have a significant impact on the overall look and feel of your outfit.

If you're wondering what color suits you, it's best to work out whether you have a cool or warm skin tone as a starting point. Then you'll be able to choose the variations of any particular color to suit your tone, for the most part, we can all wear every color, it's just about getting the right shade and how close or far from your face a particular hue sits.
