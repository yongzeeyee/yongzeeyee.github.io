Pin Boutique Sales Report

From the sales data in March, we can observe the following:

Kurung Moden had the highest quantity sold with 123 units, indicating its popularity among customers. Kurung ranked second with 72 units sold, suggesting a significant demand for this traditional wear. Kebaya and Dress had moderate sales with 60 and 56 units sold, respectively. Blouse had the lowest sales, with only 20 units sold.




Analyzing the boutique's performance over the first half of 2023, we can observe the following trends:

The number of customers steadily increased from January to March, indicating a growing customer base and potentially successful marketing strategies during that period.

March experienced the highest number of customers, reaching 544. This could be due to seasonal factors, promotional activities, or the popularity of certain products during that time.

After March, there was a slight decrease in customer numbers in April and May, followed by a moderate increase in June. Overall, the data suggests that Pin Boutique experienced significant sales in March, particularly with the popularity of Kurung Moden. Additionally, the boutique's performance showed a positive trend in terms of customer numbers, with March being the most successful month in terms of attracting customers.
